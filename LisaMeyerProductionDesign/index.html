About

Born somewhere between a shadow and a soft breeze, I began creating art shortly after misplacing a spoon and deciding it was a metaphor.

My work explores the space between thought and toast — the crackling silence of unspoken subtitles, the geometry of dreams that smell faintly of printer ink.

Educated in the School of Accidental Balance and Lightly Roasted Theories, I believe every medium has a spirit, except maybe PowerPoint.

In my spare time, I train clouds to dance in sync and archive emotional shadows cast by forgotten shopping lists.

This is not a statement. It’s a soft launch into organized confusion.
