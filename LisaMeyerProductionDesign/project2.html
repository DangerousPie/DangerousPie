Whispering Shadows in Liquid Time

2024 • Experimental Video Installation • Duration variable, space infinite

This piece dissolves the barrier between time and perception, inviting the viewer to step into a fluid moment where memory bends and light leaks through unseen cracks.

Layers of translucent imagery unfold like whispered secrets, moving in rhythms both erratic and meditative, evoking the sensation of watching a dream unravel beneath the surface of waking.

Created with a fusion of hand-drawn animation, digital glitches, and analog video feedback, the work resists fixed interpretation — instead offering a poetic space for quiet reflection and ephemeral emotion.
