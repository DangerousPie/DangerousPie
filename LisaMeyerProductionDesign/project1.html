The Persistence of Bubblewrap

2023 • Mixed Media • Approximately 17 semi-serious centimeters

This piece began as a whisper between two left shoes in a gallery without walls. The concept revolves around the transient nature of snack thoughts and the ethical implications of folding laundry during an existential crisis.

Mediums include recycled ambition, powdered stillness, and an enthusiastic misunderstanding of gravity. The work invites viewers to question whether chairs can dream of sitting down, or if that’s just projection.

Inspired by late-night toast shadows and the sound of unread emails, the process involved staring intensely at wallpaper until it blinked. The outcome: emotional scaffolding and a mild ringing in the left elbow.

Critics have described it as “definitely art” and “like a cloud, if a cloud was confused but committed.”
